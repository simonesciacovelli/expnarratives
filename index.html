Amalgam is a production studio that utilizes interactions as the base for crafting stories.

Through this balance the content produced enforces a series of mechanics that render these stories unique, allowing a variety of ways of navigating a story.

Our three primary Environments are POINT (Web), TOUCH (App), and SPACES (AR/VR). Each environment has specific functions and interactions.

A story developed for POINT will be radically different than one for SPACES

POINT utilizes the web as interaction, allowing the precision of a cursor and the capabilities of modern desktops to mesmerize

TOUCH relies on the portability of a phone, the cameras, and its malleability to amplify a story

SPACES recreates a new environment for our spectators, both mixed and virtual reality are utilized to envelop these stories

The Mechanical Wanderer is a visual examination of a system perceiving a series of worlds that move following a cyclical and designated rhythm. Each world has its peculiarities, forms, and characteristics.

The main character, in its interactive form presented in the first section of the project, is substituted by its point of view to carefully analyze these intricate worlds.

The worlds are displayed through a window that blends interactivity with storytelling, encouraging the exploration of these peculiar views

Scroll to hide the text and move the cursor to find clues or proceed to the next world

The Silent Valley of golden waves captures fragments and agitation of words devoid of sounds

The Impervious Temple that observes and directs malleable forms

The Translucent Lab holds and dissects specimens of dubious significance and provenance

The Amorphous Alps hide gems of blinding brilliance and lights of inescapable darkness

The Bewildering Ocean traps lost mariners in alluring arms and soporific stares

The Gazing Metropolis confuses its dwellers with invisible eyes and kaleidoscopic rhythms

The Rotating Stations transports visitors through ethereal tunnels and dubious destinations

The Anxious Monument glances nearby for meaning and illuminated pyramids

The Segmented Observatory traps distant screams of forgotten creatures through ages and places

The Crystal Cube conserves knowledge of no meaning, sustained by lights of monotonous resonance

Amalgam is an opportunity to create stories designed to be experienced in a virtual environment, whether that is a website, an application, or a digital space.

Through interactions and storytelling techniques the content produced enforces a series of mechanics that render these stories unique, allowing a variety of interactions and modes of navigating a story.

Storytelling is redesigned for an audience that is willing to be an active element of the development of a story, creating a personal, captivating, and impactful experience.
